FOOD AND NUTRITION

WHY DO WE NEED FOOD?

We get all the important nutrients and minerals from healthy food.
Vegetables, fruits, dairy products, and dry fruits are part of healthy food. Dairy products such as milk, eggs, ghee,
butter, and cotton cheese are rich sources of protein. Healthy food keeps our mind and body fit.
